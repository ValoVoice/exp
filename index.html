Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ValVoice

Is ValVoice safe to use with Valorant?

Yes, ValVoice is completely safe to use. It doesn't interfere with Valorant or trigger any anti-cheat systems. Our software operates by reading the chat log file, which is a safe and permitted method.

Does ValVoice affect game performance?

ValVoice is designed to be lightweight and efficient. It runs in the background with minimal resource usage, ensuring no impact on your game performance or FPS.

Can I customize the voices?

Absolutely! ValVoice offers 12+ built-in voices to choose from. Premium users also get access to exclusive Agent voices. Additionally, you can use any Windows voice on your system for free.

How do I install ValVoice?

Installation is simple: download the installer from our website, run it, and follow the step-by-step instructions. The entire process takes less than a minute.
